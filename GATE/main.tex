Three unbiased coins were tossed. Provided that at least two outcomes are tails,the probability of having all three outcomes as tails is\\
\solution \\
\begin{table}[!ht]
\centering
\input{tables/table.tex}
\caption{Definition of $X$ and parameters.}
\end{table}
\begin{align}
p_X(k) &= \frac{\comb{3}{k}}{8}\\
F_X\brak{k} &= \Pr(X \leq k)\\
&= \sum_{k=0}^k p_X(k)\\
\implies F_X(k) &= \sum_{k=0}^{k}\frac{\comb{3}{k}}{8}\\
\Pr(X \geq k) &= 1-F_X\brak{k-1}\\
\implies \Pr(X \geq 2) &= 1-F_X \brak{1}\\
&= 1-\frac{1}{2}\\
&=\frac{1}{2}\\
\pr{X=3|X \geq 2} &= \frac{\Pr(X \geq 2 \quad\&\quad X=3)}{\Pr(X \geq 2)}\\
&= \frac{\Pr(X=3)}{\Pr(X \geq 2)}\\
&= \frac{\brak{\frac{1}{8}}}{\brak{\frac{1}{2}}}\\
&= \frac{1}{4}
\end{align}
$\therefore$ The probability of having all three outcomes as tails is 0.25.
